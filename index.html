WHO WE ARE

We Offer Live Virtual Experiences With Natives Spanish Speakers

We provide tools for teachers and Spanish Language Schools, Institutes and Universities. We offer an authentic connection with Native Spanish Speakers in the form of live virtual tour, cultural experiences, gastronomic experiences, Spanish Language Immersion , customizable talks about different topics while we introduce vocabulary. Our goal is to make an outstanding complement for your Spanish Class.

Our team is formed by professionals, they are qualified Local Guides and Spanish Teachers, adding a plus to the lessons. Besides our carriers, we traveled extensively, having a deep knowledge of the Spanish and Latin-American culture.

Live Virtual Experiences

City tours in more than 50 cities all over the world, Museums, World Heritage Sites, Football Experiences, Economics, History, Culinary Experiences and Customized experiences

Spanish Conversation Club

Talks – Learn Spanish with series, movies and books. A live virtual experience will have a topic and a methodology that will be shared with incumbents in advance, and that will help them anticipate and prepare their participation.

Spanish Immersion

Explore South America with our personalized Spanish immersion trips designed for groups of students. The programs are customizable and can be combined with events, tours, excursions, different types of accommodations, cultural activities, etc.
